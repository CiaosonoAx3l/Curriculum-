My practical experiences in the IT world

My Experiences

Over the years, I have had the opportunity to develop various practical skills in the IT sector. Some of these experiences were born from real jobs, others from personal projects and the curiosity to delve into how technology works.

A large part of my skills comes from independent exploration, experimentation, and the desire to fully understand the functioning of hardware and software systems.

Waiter

During my journey, I also worked as a waiter. This experience allowed me to develop important interpersonal and organizational skills, learning to manage dynamic situations and to work in direct contact with the public.

  • Direct customer relationship
  • Teamwork
  • Stress management in dynamic environments
  • Organization and speed of service

PC Assembly and Optimization

One of the most important experiences in my journey has been building custom computers. It all started with my first self-assembled PC during high school.

Over time I have continued to deepen my knowledge in this area, studying hardware compatibility, cooling systems, BIOS configurations, and performance optimization.

  • Building PCs from scratch
  • BIOS and operating system configuration
  • Hardware performance optimization
  • Consulting for price/performance builds

Web Development

During my university studies, I started developing websites and digital applications using various technologies.

I mainly focused on creating responsive websites, databases, and modern user interfaces with custom CSS.

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Creation of responsive websites
  • Database management

Personal Projects

In addition to my university studies, I developed several personal projects to improve my technical skills.

  • Personal websites
  • Experimentation with databases
  • Game development with Unity
  • Creation of a personal NAS

Erasmus Experience

During my third year of university, I participated in the Erasmus program in Waterford, Ireland.

This experience allowed me to engage with an international context, improve my English, and broaden my vision of the technology sector.

  • University studies abroad
  • Collaboration with international students
  • Improvement of the English language
